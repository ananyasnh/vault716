Vault 716 Culinary Subscriptions

Terms & Food Safety

1. Pickup & Location Policy
Vault 716 operates as a weekly pickup service. A central convenient point will be selected for the pickups. Delivery is also available at your doorstep with an additional $15 fee. Distribution schedules are managed privately inside the member WhatsApp group. Members must pick up or receive orders within the designated window.

2. Missed Pickups
Due to the fresh preparation of ingredients, missed pickups are non-refundable and will be forfeited. Repeated missed pickups will result in subscription suspension.

3. Payment & Billing
Subscriptions are billed weekly on a cash-only on pickup basis. Pausing or changing plans must be submitted by Friday before the upcoming Monday cycle.

4. Ingredient & Allergen Disclaimer
Meals are prepared in a facility that handles poultry, dairy, nuts, wheat, soy, and gluten. We cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free environment. Consuming our food prep is at your own health risk.

5. Food Storage & Safety
Meals must be kept refrigerated at or below 40°F (4°C) and consumed within 5 days of pickup, or frozen immediately. Vault 716 accepts no responsibility for food safety after collection.
